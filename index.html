Meet Ngozi Oghenefego Chiadika

Ngozi is a digital marketer/ fashion entrepreneur who specializes in digital strategy, content creation, storytelling and fashion designing.
She has been a social media manager for a number of brands, where she curated contents for her clients.
She discovered her love for fashion as a teenager and resolved in heart to pursue fashion designing,she was resilient in her resolve and went ahead to learn the art of fashion designing after which she started a clothing line.
Ngozi believes that everything is possible if you work hard and remain consistent.
